Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

If you are a California resident, the **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)** and the **California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)** grant you the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information.

Our Policy

We **do not** directly sell your personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) for monetary value. However, like most online businesses, we use services such as advertising networks, analytics providers, and social media platforms, which may be deemed a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under CCPA/CPRA, particularly regarding data collected through cookies and tracking technologies.

Your Right to Opt-Out

You have the right to direct us not to sell or share your personal information at any time.

**To submit an opt-out request for the use of cookies and tracking technologies (used for cross-context behavioral advertising):**

  • You can adjust your preferences via our **Cookie Consent Management Platform** (if available on the site).
  • You can enable the **Global Privacy Control (GPC)** signal on your web browser. If we detect this signal, we will automatically honor your request to opt-out of the sale and sharing of your personal information.
  • You can use the form below or contact us directly at: privacy@yourcompany.com.

Submitting a Request

To formally exercise your right to opt-out, please click the link below or send an email to the address provided above. We will process your request within the legally required timeframe.

*Note: This opt-out applies to the selling/sharing of personal information and does not restrict us from transferring personal information to our service providers.*
